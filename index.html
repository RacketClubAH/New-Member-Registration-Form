Annual membership is available to Cyprus residents only, is granted at the sole discretion of the Club, and is valid for 12 consecutive months from the agreed start date. Membership becomes active only upon receipt of full payment of all applicable fees, automatically expires at the end of the 12-month period unless renewed, is personal to the applicant, and may not be transferred, assigned, shared or lent to any third party.

All membership fees are payable in full upon registration and are strictly non-refundable. No refunds, credits or extensions will be granted for non-attendance, illness, relocation, travel restrictions or other personal circumstances. Membership fees, structure and benefits are subject to annual review and may change upon renewal.

Membership benefits include clay or hard-court hire subject to availability, a maximum of 1 hour court use per day per member, 7 days advance booking, members-only events throughout the year organized by the Racket Club, and a 30% discount on padel court use off the official published rate. Padel court use is not included in the membership and no priority booking access applies.

Members may reserve courts up to 7 days in advance, may hold a maximum of 2 active bookings at any one time, and may book up to 1 hour per day per member. Additional same-day bookings may be permitted subject to availability and Club management discretion. Membership does not guarantee court availability at all times, particularly during peak periods, coaching programs or events.

The Aphrodite Hills Coaching Team retains priority access to courts at all times. During peak periods, including April, May, July, August and October, court allocation may be restricted due to coaching programs and events. The Club also reserves the right to close or restrict access to facilities for maintenance, repairs, tournaments, events, weather conditions or operational requirements, without compensation or refund.

Each member may invite a maximum of one guest per session. Two members may each invite one guest and share the same court. Guest fees are €10 per hour per player for tennis, including hard and clay courts, must be settled before play, and each individual guest is limited to a maximum of 5 visits per calendar year regardless of which member invites them.

Court bookings and all coaching sessions, including private and group lessons, must be cancelled no less than 24 hours before the scheduled start time. Late cancellations or failure to attend without notice may result in 100% of the applicable fee being charged. Where the Club cancels a booking or session, a rescheduled session or reasonable-time credit may be offered instead of a cash refund. Any waiver due to exceptional circumstances remains at the sole discretion of Club management and may require supporting documentation.

Members must check in at Reception before play, wear appropriate tennis attire and non-marking footwear, and use all facilities and equipment responsibly. Jeans, beachwear, outdoor shoes, flip flops and playing without a shirt are not permitted on court. Only the Aphrodite Hills Coaching Team may provide coaching on site, and any external coaching, paid or unpaid, is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate termination of membership without refund.

The Club may suspend or terminate membership without prior notice in cases of serious breach, non-payment, misconduct, abusive behaviour, damage to property, breach of rules or behaviour deemed detrimental to the Club.
